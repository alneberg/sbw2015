About SBW 2015

The Swedish Bioinformatics Workshop is a conference mainly directed towards PhD students
from all fields included in the broad term bioinformatics. Aside from presentations from
PhD students and young scientists, prominent scientists will also give keynote lectures.

Keynote lecturers Lennart Martens and Kári Stefánsson

We are very excited to present the two prominent scientists Dr. Lennart Martens and Dr. Kári Stefánsson for this years Swedish Bioinformatics Workshop.


Dr. Lennart Martens, at Ghent University, focuses on computational and data integration tools across multiple omics fields.

Dr. Kári Stefánsson is the founder and CEO of deCODE, the icelandic company world leading in whole-genome sequencing and investigating human mutations. Dr. Kári Stefánsson led the research effort which recently presented the largest study of whole-genome data ever published.
